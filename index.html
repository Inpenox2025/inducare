Designed for High-Volume Workflows

icare provides a comprehensive suite of cloud modules engineered to remove bottlenecks in clinic and hospital workflows, improving data accuracy and team efficiency.

🩺

Clinical OS & AI-EMR

Digitize doctor check-ups, diagnostics, and patient files within a highly responsive clinic workflow. Structured for operational speed.

  • Electronic Health Records (EHR): Maintain patient profiles, allergen files, vitals log, and clinical notes in a centralized database view.
  • Discharged Registry Log: Access historical files, cases sheets, and clinical notes for discharged patients instantly.
  • Doctor Visit Telemetry: Log check-ups per bed room with real-time temperature, heart rate, and BP tracking.
🏨

Bed Allocation & Ward Operations

Optimize room strength, ward availability, and admissions to track daily workflows with absolute precision.

  • Multi-Patient Capacity: Configure custom bed capacities (strength) per room to host multiple patients in shared wards.
  • Extra Room Services Billing: Add itemized charges for specific treatments, consumable kits, and syringes to patient invoices.
  • Uptime Occupancy Tracker: Live visual updates of active counts (e.g. 1/2 beds occupied) and availability states.
💳

Invoicing, Dues & Security

Ensure zero revenue leakage with automatic discharge blocks and transactions verification logs.

  • Discharge Revenue Guard: Automatically blocks room discharge if a patient has outstanding invoice dues.
  • Reconciliation Ledger: Sync billing ledgers against cash, card, and digital collections dynamically.
  • Dynamic PDF Itemizer: Generate professional invoice receipts detailing room fees and specific patient services.
