Designed for High-Volume Workflows

Inducare provides a comprehensive suite of cloud modules engineered to remove bottlenecks in clinic and hospital workflows, improving data accuracy and team efficiency.

👤

EHR & Patient Registry

Maintain centralized electronic health records. Access clinical history, manage past diagnoses, track active treatments, and ensure continuity of patient care.

📅

Smart Scheduling Queue

Coordinate appointments, assign consulting doctors, track real-time check-in statuses, and minimize wait times using dynamic scheduling queues.

💸

Automated Invoice Billing

Create itemized invoices, calculate outstanding balances, specify payment statuses, and instantly generate print-ready PDF receipts signed by your clinic.

👮

Role-Based Access (RBAC)

Secure patient confidentiality. Assign specialized permissions for administrators, doctors, and staff members, limiting view/edit access to authorized roles.

📈

Reconciliation & Analytics

Track collections by cash, card, and digital modes. Reconcile daily transactions and audit invoice discrepancies through clean dashboard visualizations.
